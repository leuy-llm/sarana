Welcome to Sinaka
Angkor Hotel

Located on the east side of Siem Reap, in a nice quiet area, still close to city center, Sinaka Angkor hotel offers a contemporary-styled accommodation with free WiFi throughout our property.

Our hotel features an outdoor swimming pool, an in-house restaurant and a bar. Our spacious, air-conditioned rooms are fitted with two queen-size beds and can accommodate up to 4 adults, with amenities that include coffee/tea making facilities, mini fridge, wardrobe and a flat-screen cable TV. Two complimentary bottles of water are replenished daily. We also provide room service. Selected apartment suites also feature a kitchenette, and balcony.
